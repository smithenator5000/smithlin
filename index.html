smithlin

What is This?

smithlin is a basic but inituive C++ library for linear algebra. It is currently in development by yours truly, Smithenator5000. It aims to support standard linear algebra objects such as matrices and vectors, in a way that is not too disimilar to how one would write it on pape. For example, as opposed to writing matrix product AB as product(A, B) (or worse yet, using a nested 'for' loop), one may write A*B.

What Can It Do?

It is early days at the moment but already, smithlin can:
  • Do basic matrix calculations, such as adding and multiplying matrices.
  • Find matrix determinants with two different methods.
  • Do basic vector operations such as the dot and cross product.
  • And a little bit more . . .
Like I said, it's early days.
